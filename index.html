RECOGNITION & AFFILIATION

Accurate Institute Of Polytechnic is affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Lucknow and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

ABOUT THREE YEARS OF DIPLOMA

Accurate Institute Of Polytechnic offers Three Year Diploma courses which are popular as Polytechnic diploma programme. Duration of the program is 3 Years. However, there is provision of lateral entry in II year to I.T.I diploma holders (10% seats are reserved in each branch, except CS/IT for ITI diploma Holders). The course aims at students who wish to make their career in the domain of Technology. After the successful completion of the courses, students can make career in the same.

ABOUT ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (Eligibility for Admission in Three Year Diploma Programs (Polytechnic Courses) as per B.T.E., Lucknow norms)

The Candidates should have passed 10th standard examination from a recognized Board of Education with a minimum of 35 percent of marks.ITI Diploma holders are eligible for direct entry into II year (10% seats in each branch may be allocated to such cases, if required).
